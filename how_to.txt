To Begin first select the source KML file by clicking 'Open KML.'

Next choose the destination to which the new KML will be saved, by clicking, 'Save KML.'

Then choose the destination to which the resulting image file will be saved by clicking, 'Save Image.'

If no destination file is selected, then the choice of open kml will be overwritten during operation.

If neither the destination of open, or save image is chosen, the procedure will prompted you to do so.

Next, input the appropriate fields to be used in the procedure;
     u'\u2022'    Latitude of Center: latitude of center point of focused sub-portion.
     u'\u2022'    Longitude of Center: longitude of center point of focused sub-portion.
     u'\u2022'    Zoom: Relative level of perspective zoom, google api depenedent.
     u'\u2022'    Image Size: distance, in miles, spanning the desired region of restriction.

Finally click run, and wait as the operations are executed.
